iParade

Instructions

iParade is a locative multimedia experience. To participate in this journey, you will walk to several nearby locations to access uploaded segments that are linked to each particular location.

The MAP tab will show your current location and the locations you need to visit to complete the journey. The next place you need to visit will be shown with a green marker . The map will re-center on your current location each time you go the map tab.

To see the introduction page (and other content), visit the HOME tab . After you read the introduction page tap the NEXT button .

The HOME tab is where you can read the text and see the video for each location.

After you finish reading the text press the PLAY button . This button will appear once the video is fully loaded. Until that time you will see a LOADING animation .

When the video is finished playing you will need to tap “done” in your media player (on some devices) and then NEXT to continue. Then, go to the next location that is marked in green in your MAP tab and continue to view content and visit sites until the last location.

You have the option to repeat/reload one step or the entire App . You may need to reload a step, if the LOADING animation continues to show for a very long time and the PLAY button never appears.

In the SETTINGS tab you can turn the voice-over off or on by tapping the SOUND button . The two repeat/reload buttons: one step or the entire App , are also available in the SETTINGS tab .

On some devices you can hit the BACK button to quit the app.

For additional information about the inspiration for and creation of this App, please visit the NOTES tab .

Notes

iParade is a series of locative media-works, created by LoVid as smart-phone Apps. iParade uses GPS data and includes video, sound, and texts that are accessible only in specific geographic locations. The content is inspired by, and recorded in the same locations, such as a street, a building, or a city park. To watch the work in full, visitors need to physically relocate themselves to access uploaded segments that are linked to each particular location. iParade renews viewers’ appreciation of their physical environment by providing a unique perspective into narratives and abstractions of public spaces.
