Speculation
Walking to the estimated location he anticipated a vastness that will feel like the radius of somewhere familiar. 
Filled with pre-conceived ideas he reached a featureless house smoothed by time.  
The decay of details and of human effort now renders it timeless. 
Thankfully, this will not be hard to erase - null object.

Even now he could feel the effacement of all his materials: woven glass, copper, calcium, conductive tissues.
The stones, the façade, project emptiness. 
The core of the banister, the deep soil: still void of human intention.

The space of the house that was here is vast, bigger now in it’s absence.
The screen could accurately illustrate its dimensions with archived documents and restored historical drawings.
Visitors walk around it with data slicing magnifying glasses.
But this will not assist sensing the scale of the house that moves.
