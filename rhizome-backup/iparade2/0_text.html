iParade#2: Unchanged When Exhumed

Welcome and thanks for joining us.
Unchanged When Exhumed will take you on a short walk in the historic neighborhood of Hamilton Heights.
This App includes an experimental multi-media video inspired by the locations you will visit.

To begin experiencing the App you need to physically go to the destination marked in green on your MAP tab . The first location is near West 140th Street and Convent Avenue. For more detailed instructions visit the INSTRUCTIONS tab .

iParade#2: Unchanged When Exhumed has 5 short sections each located within a couple of blocks of each other. The estimated time to experience the entire piece is 45 minutes.

Once the content has finished loading, hit the NEXT button below then visit the first location to begin the journey.
