iParade#3: Disintegrative Fabrication

Welcome and thanks for joining us.

Disintegrative Fabrication includes experimental locative video and text inspired by the locations you will visit from College Avenue to Downtown Troy.

The work mixes historical facts, serendipitous encounters, and speculative futures.

To start your experience, you need to physically go to the destination marked in green on your MAP tab. The first destination is near College Ave and 13th Street. For more detailed instructions visit the INSTRUCTIONS tab .

iParade#3: Disintegrative Fabrication has 4 sections along the path. The estimated time to experience the entire piece is 45 minutes to an hour depending on your walking and looking time.
