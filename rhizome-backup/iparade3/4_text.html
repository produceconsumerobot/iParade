Step Four

Hannah.

The house is saturated with simulations, augmented beverages, and digitally placed décor.

She looks out the window. Her data and nutrient plan has been adjusted to meet her needs and her means. She cannot go anywhere because the presets were calculated to sustain her in one place.

But today she is consumed with boredom. And something at the window catches her eye. A band of people, looking at the house. Not the house, the place where the house stands.

She can sense the space, the air between her and the wall, the wall's surface echoes in the room.

Her shirt, she remembers, is a simulation tool too.

The kind that enhances tangible prompts of a fireplace or linen.

Functionality supplemented by design.

In a moment of inspiration and disparity she cuts into it: the threads and electrofibers. She will embellish her work with buttons, fabric scraps, and an interface.

There is meaning in mythology, and the builders have calculated that variable. A mythological story of disembodiment retold.
